Scams & Etiquette Tips

  • Beware: Fake Bluebird taxis, overpriced transport—always agree on price before starting.
  • Money changers: Use only licensed ones. Count money before leaving.
  • Dress modestly: Always wear a sarong in temples.
  • Politeness: Greet with a smile, use right hand for giving/receiving.
  • Bargaining: Haggle with a smile in markets but accept ‘no’ gracefully.
  • Shoes off: Remove shoes before entering homes or certain shops.
  • Respect ceremonies: Don’t interrupt religious rituals or block processions for photos.
