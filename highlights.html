Don't Miss These Experiences!

Monkey Forest Must-See

Over 700 monkeys & ancient ruins.

Pro Tip: Go at 8am for fewer crowds. Hide your snacks!

Tegallalang Rice Terraces Instagrammable

Stunning sunrise walks among emerald paddies.

Secret: Visit the swings for epic photos.

Mount Batur Sunrise Epic

Start at 2am. Watch the sun rise above clouds.

Must do: Hot springs after the hike!

Night Market Food Tour Local Fav

Sample Babi Guling, sate, and tropical fruits at Gianyar Night Market.

Bring cash!

Nusa Penida Island Adventure

Kelingking Beach, Broken Beach, Crystal Bay.

Tip: Roads are rough, hire a driver.

Kecak Fire Dance Iconic

Fire & trance at sunset in Uluwatu Temple.

Book early for best seats!

How to Do 3 Highlights in 1 Day

Morning: Sunrise at Tegallalang Rice Terraces.
Late Morning: Stroll Ubud Monkey Forest.
Afternoon: Grab street food at Gianyar Night Market.
Evening: Watch the Kecak Fire Dance at Uluwatu.

Local Hack: Grab a scooter for the day for max flexibility (or hire a driver for comfort).
